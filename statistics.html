food price index

I carried out research on food prices in New Zealand. My team and I webscraped prices from supermarkets online shopping websites. Using this data and multilateral price index theory I made a digital food price index. The outcomes of the research were a paper presented at the Ottowwa Group, an international group of experts in price theory, and an R package which is available on Github
